Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need an internet connection for the operations?

No, the AI-powered background removal, document scanning, and face detection features run entirely on your device (offline). This makes the app very fast and ensures your privacy is protected.

Which biometric photo formats are supported?

BioScan automatically supports many international (ICAO) standard formats, such as Turkey ID/Passport/Driver's License, US Visa (2x2 inches), Schengen Visa (35x45mm), and Standard Portrait (45x60mm).

How can I share my scanned documents?

You can directly convert your multi-page scanned documents into a high-quality PDF format within the app and easily share them using the share menu (Mail, WhatsApp, etc.).

How does the OCR (Text Recognition) feature work?

By tapping the "Read Text" option, you can convert text from your scanned or uploaded documents into copyable digital text in seconds. It works with high accuracy in both English and Turkish.
